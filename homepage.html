Welcome to the vibrant world of Computer Science at the University of Ghana. Our department is a hub of innovation, exploration, and limitless possibilities in the realm of technology. As a cornerstone of the digital era, we empower our students to become adept problem solvers, creative thinkers, and trailblazers in this rapidly evolving field. At the University of Ghana we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with a solid foundation in computer science theory, programming languages, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and more. Our dedicated faculty, comprised of accomplished researchers and industry experts, mentor and guide students through hands-on projects and cutting-edge research, fostering an environment that encourages curiosity and intellectual growth. Beyond the classroom, our students engage in various extracurricular activities, hackathons, and internships that complement their academic journey, providing practical experiences that prepare them for the demands of the tech industry. Explore our programs, discover our research initiatives, and join a community passionate about shaping the future through technology. Whether you aspire to innovate, create groundbreaking software, or contribute to technological advancements, the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana is where your journey begins.

The range of courses available in the department and the skills acquired by students enable them to find employment in many diverse fields of the economy and prepare our graduates for the computing industry, working in a project team (or research and development team), and to enable the graduate to bring specialist skills to that team. The course also provides the platform for further study or research through exposure to established and emerging technologies and methods, and to active fields of research in Computer Science. The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
